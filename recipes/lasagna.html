Lasagna Recipe

  • Prep time: 30 min
  • Cook time: 30 min
  • Total time: 60 min
  • Servings: 8

  1. 9 lasagna noodles
  2. 1 pound ground beef
  3. 1 onion, chopped
  4. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. 2 cups ricotta cheese
  6. 1 egg
  7. 3 cups marinara sauce
  8. 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
