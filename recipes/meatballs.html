Meatballs Recipe

  • Prep time: 15 min
  • Cook time: 25 min
  • Total time: 40 min
  • Servings: 4

  1. 1 pound ground beef
  2. 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  3. 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  4. 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  5. 1 egg
  6. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  7. Salt and pepper to taste
